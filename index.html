The Ultimate Solution Challenge 2023

The Ultimate Solution Challenge 2023 is based on Google Solution Challenge which focuses on the 17 SDGs(Sustainable Development Goals). It would be an excellent way to engage students in developing innovative solutions to some of the world's most pressing problems. The challenge would involve teams of students working together to develop solutions that address one or more of the 17 SDGs. These solutions could include technology, policy, or social innovation, and could be targeted at local, national, or global levels. Participants would receive mentorship and support throughout the event, including access to subject matter experts, data and other resources.
